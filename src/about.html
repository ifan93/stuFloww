About stuFlow

StuFlow is an innovative and user-friendly web application designed specifically to cater to the needs of students. As a budding web developer, this project marks your first foray into the world of web development, and it's poised to make a significant impact in the lives of students looking to stay organized and on top of their tasks. Let's delve deeper into what StuFlow offers and how it can benefit students in various aspects of their academic journey.

why stuFlow?

Keep your student life organized. StuFlow helps you manage assignments, projects, and exams
